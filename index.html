NICA Weather Policy
  • 4-Level System: GREEN (proceed), YELLOW (monitor), ORANGE (modify activity), RED (cancel/delay).
  • Lightning: Activity stops when lightning is detected within 10 miles. A 30-minute wait from the last strike is required before resuming.
  • Heat (Heat Index): GREEN <95°F, YELLOW 95–100°F (max 2 hrs), ORANGE 100–105°F (max 1 hr), RED >105°F (cancel).
  • Wind: Sustained >25 mph or gusts >35 mph may require course modifications. Sustained >35 mph = cancel.
  • Heavy Rain: Heavy rain rates may require trail evacuation and shelter-in-place.
  • Air Quality (AQI): GREEN 0–50, YELLOW 51–100, ORANGE 101–150 (competitive canceled), RED >150 (all canceled).
  • Trail Conditions: Races may be cancelled if trails are too wet, in coordination with the land manager.
  • NWS Alerts: Tornado/Severe Thunderstorm/Flash Flood Warnings auto-trigger RED. Watches auto-trigger ORANGE minimum.
  • Decision by: League Director, Event Director, and Chief Course Marshal make the final call.
