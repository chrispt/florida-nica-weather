Florida NICA Race Weather

Weather Policy
  • Lightning: Activity stops when lightning is detected within 10 miles. A 30-minute wait from the last strike is required before resuming.
  • Rain/Trails: Races may be cancelled if trail conditions are too wet, in coordination with the land manager.
  • Course: Races may be cancelled if the course is un-rideable and dangerous for riders.
  • Decision by: League Director, Event Director, and Chief Course Marshal make the final call.
